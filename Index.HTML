FAQ Page

Question: How does it work?
Answer: The process of selling your client’s home subject to is similar to a traditional sale. We will do a walkthrough of the property, discuss the terms of the sale, and sign a purchase agreement. We will then take over the mortgage payments and transfer the deed to our company. Your client will receive their equity from the sale and can move on with their life.
Question: What are the benefits?
Answer: Most of the agents we are working with have clients in pre-foreclosure or low equity situations. The benefits of selling your client’s home subject include; the ability to sell quickly without them having to bring a check to closing, no appraisals, no renovations or repairs & working with an experienced buyer. It can be an excellent solution for homeowners in challenging financial situations or who need to sell their home quickly.
Question: How long does the process take?
Answer: The process of selling your client’s home Subject To can typically be completed within a few weeks, depending on the specific circumstances of the sale. We work quickly and efficiently to ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience for our clients.
Question: How do you get paid as a real estate agent?
Answer: As the buyer, we pay your real estate commission for the transaction.
Question: Do you use a servicing company to handle all the payments?
Answer: Yes, we use a professional servicing company to handle all mortgage payments and ensure that they are made on time.
Question: How does Subject To affect your seller's DTI?
Answer: Every person's financial situation is unique, but we have a method for mitigating the impact of subject to transactions on the seller's debt-to-income ratio.
Question: What is the due on sale clause & should I be worried?
Answer: The due on sale clause is a provision in a mortgage that requires the borrower to pay off the loan if the property is sold or transferred to another party. While this clause can be a concern for some sellers, we have a thorough understanding of how to navigate it and ensure that the transaction is completed smoothly.
Question: When will you pay my client's mortgage off?
Answer: The timing for paying off a client's mortgage varies based on each situation. We may keep the loan in place for 30 years. However, we typically sell, refinance, or 1031 exchange properties every 7-10 years. Additionally, our servicing company ensures that the mortgage payment falls off of the client's debt-to-income ratio.
Question: What happens if the homeowner is behind on mortgage payments?
Answer: If the homeowner is behind on mortgage payments, we can still help them in most cases. We can take over their payments and bring the mortgage current so that they can avoid foreclosure.
Question: Will my credit be affected if I sell my home subject to?
Answer: Selling your client’s home should not have a negative impact on their credit as long as the mortgage payments are made on time, which is why they need a qualified buyer ;). In fact, it may even help improve your client’s credit by reducing their debt-to-income ratio. If your client is in a pre-foreclosure situation there are even more benefits.
